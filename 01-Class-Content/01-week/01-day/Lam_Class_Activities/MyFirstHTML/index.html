How to make my Favorite Sandwich

  1. Purchase Organic Peanut Butter, Kerry Gold Salted Butter, Florida Honey, Apples and Blueberries, and Ezekial Bread from Publix
  2. Take 2 pieces of Ezekial Bread and lay them side to side
  3. On the left piece of bread spread peanut butter evenly
  4. On the left piece of bread, spread honey to taste evenly
  5. On right piece of bread, spread warmed up butter evenly
  6. Place right piece of bread onto left piece of bread with filler ingredients in the center between two pieces of bread
  7. Place sandwich on plate
  8. Crush berries and apples into paste
  9. Place crushed fruit in a dollop next to sandwich in what you percieve as an attractive manner
